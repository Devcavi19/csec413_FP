How It Works

Net Profit = Bet Amount × (Multiplier - 1)

Example: $10 bet, 2 hits → $10 × (4 - 1) = $30 profit

Game Mechanics

  • 3 balls drop sequentially per round
  • Player chooses one card to bet on
  • Payout based on balls landing on chosen card
  • Same payout structure for both game types
