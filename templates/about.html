üìú Origins and Cultural Significance

The Drop Ball Game (also known as "Color Game" variations) is a traditional Filipino carnival game commonly found in perya (local fairs). Players bet on which card (9, 10, J, Q, K, A) a ball will land on after being dropped through a maze of obstacles.

The game's simplicity and fast-paced nature make it popular among fair-goers, but like most gambling games, it involves probability mechanics that favor the house.

  • Reduced probability per card
  • Negative EV (house advantage)
